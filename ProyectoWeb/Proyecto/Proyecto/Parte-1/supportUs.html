About Us

At Faro Huellitas, we are committed to the protection and well-being of animals. Our foundation was created with the mission of rescuing, rehabilitating, and finding loving homes for animals in vulnerable situations. Our team consists of animal lovers, passionate volunteers, and dedicated professionals who are devoted to ensuring the welfare of each of our rescues. We strive to educate the community about the importance of responsible adoption, sterilization, and castration, as well as respect for all forms of life.

We encourage you to explore our website and discover how you can be part of our mission. Whether you want to adopt, donate, volunteer, or spread our message, every small contribution counts and brings us closer to our goal: a world where all animals are valued, respected, and loved.
